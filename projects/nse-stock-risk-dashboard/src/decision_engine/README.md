# Decision Engine

This module will convert statistical signals and user trade inputs into stakeholder-facing decisions.

Inputs:

- Stock risk metrics
- User capital
- Risk per trade percent
- Entry price
- Stop-loss price
- Target price

Outputs:

- Suggested quantity
- Potential loss
- Potential reward
- Risk-reward ratio
- Decision label
- Explanation

Decision labels:

- Avoid
- Watchlist
- Small Position
- Normal Position
- Reduce Risk
